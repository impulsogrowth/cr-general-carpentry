Formats

Content Format Priority

Meta pushes different formats differently. Here's how to prioritize based on what drives reach vs. engagement vs. leads.

🎬

Reels — #1 for Reach

Highest organic reach of any format. Before/afters and process videos perform best. 15–45 seconds, trending audio, no text needed.

📊

Carousels — #1 for Saves

Most saved format = best for algorithm long-term. Education, project walkthroughs, tips. First slide must stop the scroll.

📸

Photos — #1 for Brand

Detail shots and finished rooms build brand quality perception. Less reach than Reels but important for the overall feed aesthetic.

📖

Stories — #1 for Retention

Keeps you top of mind with existing followers. Daily touchpoint. Not about reach — about staying in the conversation.

Captions

Caption Formula That Converts

Every caption follows the same 5-part structure. Takes 3 minutes to write once you know the formula.

1
Hook (first line — make them tap "more") "This kitchen went from builder-grade to jaw-dropping in 3 weeks." / "Most people waste money on the wrong upgrade first."
2
The story (2–3 sentences) What was the project, what was the challenge, what did we deliver. Keep it simple and specific — real details build trust.
3
Value or insight (1–2 sentences) One thing they can learn or take away. "The secret to a kitchen that looks expensive? Cabinet hardware. It's 5% of the budget and changes everything."
4
Question or engagement prompt "Which finish would you choose — matte black or brushed gold?" / "Tag someone who needs a kitchen upgrade 👇"
5
CTA + Contact "Ready to start yours? DM us or call 786-803-6784 for a free estimate. Miami & surrounding areas." Then hashtags below.
